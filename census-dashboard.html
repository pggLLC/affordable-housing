Census Multifamily Lens

Pulls American Community Survey (ACS) housing profile indicators and lets you switch between national, state, and local geographies to support multifamily market analysis.

Geography

Multifamily share (Units in structure)

ACS DP04 percent of housing units in buildings with 5–9, 10–19, and 20+ units.

Total housing units

ACS DP04 total housing units (estimate) for the selected geography.

How this helps multifamily analysis

  • Supply composition: higher 5+ unit shares typically correlate with stronger apartment market depth.
  • Site selection: compare metros/counties/places for multifamily penetration before underwriting rents and absorption.
  • LIHTC context: combine this with your allocation/QAP insights and pipeline indicators (FRED) to see where affordable supply fits.
Data: Census Bureau ACS 1-year profile (DP04). Variables are documented in the Census API DP04 group reference.
