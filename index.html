Affordable Housing Market Intelligence Powered by LIHTC Analytics

The Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) program is the primary driver of affordable rental housing production in the United States. This platform provides data-driven insights into how policies, allocation, and market conditions influence where and how affordable housing is built.

Built for

  • Affordable housing developers
  • Tax credit investors and syndicators
  • Housing finance agencies
  • Planners and community development professionals

Why this Matters

While LIHTC is federally authorized, each state allocates credits differently through its Qualified Allocation Plan (QAP). These policy differences create distinct development environments, risk profiles, and investment outcomes across states. States design their own allocation priorities even though credits originate federally. (Source: Congress.gov)
